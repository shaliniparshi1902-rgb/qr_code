# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Respect different levels of experience and background.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that do not follow this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.
